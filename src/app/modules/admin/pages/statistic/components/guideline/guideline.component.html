FTTH Reuse/ Revoke Procedure Guide

I. Main Process

# Step 1

HO assigns targets to each province and distributes them to individual D2D and FBB Leaders.

# Step 2

Coordinate D2D staff to approach customers home and encourage them to make payment to Reuse or Revoke ONU.

If the customer agrees to make payment and continue using the service (Reuse):

Prompt the customer to make a payment with good promotion and good quality internet.

Once the payment is completed, update the status to "Reuse" in the system.

If the customer does not agree to make payment (Revoke):

Retrieve the ONU device from the customer HOME

Update the tool status to "ONU Revoke on link.

II. Guideline to use the tool

# Step 1

ACCESS LINK: https://account.mytel.com.mm/fbb-onu/#/login

# Step 2

Type VMY CODE and log in with OTP.

III. Guideline for FBB Leader or BCM approve

# Step 1

FBB Leader/ BCM click on tab Customers.

# Step 2

Click to filter >> choose “REVOKE”.

IV. UPDATE ONU REUSE/REVOKE STATUS

Step 3 : ONU Device Retrieval Process

# Step 3.1

Update the "ONU REVOKE" status in the management software.

# Step 3.2

The FBB Leader terminates the contract on the bccs system with the reason “KHYC-Terminate process”.

# Step 3.3

The FBB leader Terminate ticket will receive to partner staff and they need to finish terminate task in MBCCS with the reason “Withdrawal Good”.

# Step 3.4

At the end of each day, D2D staff and the FBB Leader sign the ONU handover record and deliver it to the BC warehouse.

# Step 3.5

When BC receive ONU physical, BCM will go to portal and click on the portal list revoked and choose BUTTON “RECEIVED ONU”.

# Step 3.6

The BC warehouse forwards the ONU device to the branch warehouse.

# Step 3.7

The branch warehouse consolidates and sends the ONU devices to the HO warehouse for centralized management.

Note that:

When D2D go to customers and collect ONU should ask customers pay the debt remain first to request reuse, if customers not agree check with customers which time set up internet, Policy: if the customers using less than 6 months and request Terminate contract > Customers have to pay 50,000 MMK.

If customers have debt, and customers don’t wanna use again Mytel Wifi, D2D Collect ONU back and update on the system. For the debt, Mytel HO will base on the receive ONU (physical received) Submit statement for clear that debt.

This procedure ensures accurate, transparent retrieval of ONU modems, and guarantees that devices are retrieved and stored according to proper regulations.

V. GUIDELINE PROCESS FOR PAYMENT INCENTIVE FOR D2D

1 ONU RECEIVED SUCCESS PHYSICAL CONFIRM BY BRANCH (FBB LEADER, DBC, BRANCH DIRECTOR) WILL RECEIVE 2,000 BONUS.

End of the month, Branch will base on the list receive ONU of system and physical receive, will submit the list ONU receive via Voffice to send to HO before 4th of the month , sign by Branch Director. After that, HO will calculate the Incentive base on that file.
